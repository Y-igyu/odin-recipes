Creamy Chicken Piccata Pasta Bake

This creamy chicken piccata pasta bake is bright with lemon and capers, creamy and cheesy with Alfredo pasta sauce, ricotta, and Parmesan. The prep time to make this meal is 15 minutes and cook time is 50 minutes for a total time of 1 hour and 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2(15-ounce) jars Alfredo sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 1(3-ounce) jar capers, drained
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 1/2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh Italian parsley, or as needed
