Instant Pot Pot Roast

Instant Pot Pot Roast with simple ingredients, you can have a flavorful, comforting pot roast in less time than using an oven or slow cooker. Prep time takes about 20 minutes and cook time is about 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 pound baby potatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups baby carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 packet dry onion soup mix
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heet 2 tablespoons oil in a multi-functional pressure cooker set to the Saute fucntion. Add roast to hot oil and cook until browned all over, 4 to 5 minutes per side. Pour in broth.
  3. Combine potatoes, carrots, onion, soup mix, and remaining 2 tablespoons oil in a resealable plastic bag. Seal bag and shake until vegetables are evenly coated.
  4. Pour mixture into the pressure cooker and spread out evenly.
  5. Close and lock lid. Select high pressure according to the manufacturer's instructions. Set timer for 60 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for the pressure to build. Release the pressure carefully using quick-release method according to the manufacturer's instructions, about 5 minutes. Unlock and remove lid.
  6. Transfer roast and vegetables to a serving platter, leaving drippings in the pot.
  7. Select the Saute function and bring dripping to a boil. Combine water and cornstarch in a bowl and pour into the drippings. Cook, stirring frequently, until grave is slightly thickened, 2 to 3 minutes. Season with garlic salt and pepper. Slice roast and serve with vegetables and gravy.
